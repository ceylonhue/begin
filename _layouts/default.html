Why I.T? Why now?

The reason i want to study I.T is because I relate to it easily having grown up with the industry. I feel although, quite advanced in terms of what the industry has achieved, the field is still quite young and there is much more to be discovered and m any more milestones to reach.

My interest into I.T came when my family got our first computer, it quickly turned into my hobby. Later learning that I.T is not just about computers and that it affects our everday lives I was intrigued. After learning about automation, coding and all the while enjoying the practical benefits from innovations such as email, telephones, internet and security systems I knew that I.T was the future or as I see it, I.T will build the future.

After high school I worked in a call center for an insurance company. I got to see first hand the transition from the company using "claim forms" to lodge insurance claims to all claims being lodged either online by the Policy Holder or by the claims consulatant over the phone. The new claims lodgement options eliminated countless steps for both parties involved. This is one of the reasons I looked into Software engineering and furthering my knowledge and IT.

I didnt waste time, I was able to get a position within the same company as a software tester for the new claims system. This really let me see the workflow and the environment in which the I.T industry possibly operates. I did this for about 2 years and then went to travel in Japan.

After an eye opening year eating ramen and sushi which im yet to get sick of ! I'm back a nd i want to pursuit my passion and career once more. However being a bit older and having less time my options were limited. RMIT offered online courses which suits me perfectly.

I want to broaden my knowledge in the field of I.T from this course and use this time to figure out if Software engineering is what i want to do or something similar within I.T.
