Why I.T? Why now?

The reason i want to study I.T is because I relate to it easily having grown up with the industry. I feel although, quite advanced in terms of what the industry has achieved, the field is still quite young and there is much more to be discovered and m any more milestones to reach.

My interest into I.T came when my family got our first computer, it quickly turned into my hobby. Later learning that I.T is not just about computers and that it affects our everday lives I was intrigued. After learning about automation, coding and all the while enjoying the practical benefits from innovations such as email, telephones, internet and security systems I knew that I.T was the future or as I see it, I.T will build the future.

MB test is somewhat of a self reflection test, a psychological type of personality test. The questions revolve around 4 main categories which then determines which personality you. Emotional intelligence Test - Talentsmart.com


Emotional intelligence is how a person handles his emotions and different situations and how aware he is of it, not only that but the ability to adapt or simple change the way you act or feel in relation to someone elses emotions, thats how i would explain it but in short... the awareness and measure of how emotions affect emotions. This E.I test was the longest out of the 3 tests and had some very interesting questions that really gets you thinking in a philisophical way. I took my time doing this test and it seems my E.I is fairly average maybe even slightly above. With a score of 74 out of 100. In a nutshell my test results indicate that Im aware of some the thingss that i need to improve on but not everything. This isnt the end of the world and many people with similar scores to me are able use this test to improve weak traits to make BIG improvements. Im hoping the same, only time will tell...

Mock Project - One Stop Tutor Hire

Overview
Design a system that can be a universal access point for finding Skilled tutors in your area. A system that can be a contact point between those who wish to gain knowledge and those who want to offer it. Similiar ride sharing app's, you can find tutors in your area who have indicated they are available. Unlike ride shares the experience with the customer is much more hands on and interactive. The system will show what are tutors available sorted by categories. Each tutor "profile" will have their availability displayed and as well prices. Customers are able to leave feedback regarding their lessons.

Motivation
Due to the frustration from myself and many i have known regarding the process of getting a tutor in regards to music in particular is quite dated and inefficent. When i was younger and looking for a guitar teacher i tried all the normal routes like going to the local music shop, looking in the yellow pages, using the internet, asking friends.

After finding one or several, there is no way I knew who to go with or if they were any good. All i had was word of mouth or pure luck. To be able to have a larger "community" in one where others can leave reviews and tutors can place updates. Think Uber meets Skillshare meets Music.


System Features
One DataeBase - The system will be able to store all tutor information under the same system to allow for a universal point of contact for anyone seeking skilled tutors.
br> Multiple OS Access
The system will be cross platform over iOS apps, android and PC to allow for greater visibility and accesebility and convienience. On all platforms you will be able to access the same database community.
br> User and Tutor Logins and Profiles
The system will require a login to a registered user to keep track of all information. Users and tutors will also have user profiles just like on most mediums these days. Profiles allow for sharing of updates, a point of contact between the community. Tutors can also upload any videos or documents onto their profile.

Transactions through the App
Tutors can offer their services and skills in the form of videos or documents. This is another source of income for the tutors but also another form of convieience for user. In the cases Tutors are not available or the user does not wish to meet 'face to face', the option is still available to learn from tutors.

Rating system
Users will be able to rate their lessons, give feedback in form of stars which will give tutors a score. This is one of the most important features. Besides word of mouth at the moment there is no way to easily know how good a person is at teaching their skill, My aim would be to build up a large enough database and commmunity that this is no longer a frustration.

Easy to start
Tutors will no longer need to take out ads in papers, on facebook or regulalry visitting their local shop.

Community Driven
Some of the hardest parts about learning a skill is the fear of failing or feeling like you have no support or wether you are even doing it correctly. Having a teacher helps with this, but sometimes its even too daunting to let an 'expert' in that field know how you really feel. THe community that will build up from the app usage from people leaving feedback on tutors and commenting on videos or just chatting to each other, the community will act as a "friend" in your learning journey to help ease the times when you are doubting.

Advertisements
The app will feature ads as a source of revenue and outreach. Because I aim to include all types of skills and not just one field. Relations with all those fields would undoubletley form, which in future i hope will allow for the fine tuning of criteria's within our database searches to further provide for the customer.

Skills Needed
App design , UI design, Product design, data science, User research, product mangement

Final Outcome
Finding capable people to pass on their skills will be as easy as ordering an uber. The feeling of not being able to learn enough from that one youtube tutorial will be old news. With fast turn around response times, availabilty info, and tutors offline materials. There will always be an option to find the lessons you want.
